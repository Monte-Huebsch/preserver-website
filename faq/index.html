Privacy & Data
Where is my data stored?
On your device, by default. If you choose, you can store your Activities in your personal cloud account (Google Drive, iCloud, Dropbox, etc.). You're always in control — nothing is shared with anyone unless you actively choose to share it.
Do you share my data with third parties?
Never. Preserver does not sell, share, or use your data for AI training. No data is accessible to anyone but you. No ad platform has access to your captures or personal data.
Can I use AI tools on my captured data?
Yes — through your phone's native AI capabilities. Preserver stores your captures privately on your device. You can then use any AI app installed on your phone to review, search or summarise that data. Preserver itself does not include or process AI features.
Can Preserver see my captures?
No. Your captures are stored on your device and, if you choose, in your own cloud account. Preserver as a company has no access to what you record. There is no server where your content is uploaded to us.
What happens to my data if I delete the app?
Your data is stored on your device, so deleting the app removes it from that device. If you've backed up to a personal cloud account (Google Drive, iCloud, etc.), your captures remain there under your control.
Features & Capture
What types of media can I capture?
Text notes, voice recordings, photos, and video — all within a single Activity entry. Everything is automatically stamped with date, time and GPS at the moment of capture.
What metadata is captured automatically?
Every capture is automatically stamped with date, time, and GPS coordinates. Unlike most apps, this metadata stays embedded in your data even when you export or share it — so your proof of time and location travels with the record.
Does it work without an internet connection?
Absolutely. Preserver works completely offline — no Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or mobile data required. GPS capture works via satellite with no network connection at all. Your captures are stored on your device until you choose to back them up or share them.
Does GPS work without mobile signal?
Yes. GPS positioning uses satellites, not mobile towers or Wi-Fi. It works underground, in aircraft, in remote areas, and anywhere else there is no mobile or data signal. This is one of Preserver's most important features for field users.
What is an Activity?
An Activity is a container for a related set of captures — for example, a job site visit, a field research session, or a day's notes. Within each Activity you can mix text, voice, photos and video, all sharing the same timestamp and location context.
How do I export or share my captures?
From within any Activity, you can share directly to other apps, export to your cloud storage, or send via email or messaging. You control exactly what gets shared and with whom. The GPS and timestamp metadata travels with the export.
Can I add notes after the fact?
Yes, you can add additional captures to an Activity at any time. The original captures retain their original timestamps, so the record of when each item was captured is always accurate and tamper-evident.

Professional & Compliance Use
Can I use Preserver for professional or compliance purposes?
Yes. Preserver is used across 40+ industries for timestamped evidence, field records, compliance documentation, and more. The automatic GPS and timestamp metadata makes it suitable for evidence-grade capture in many professional contexts.
Is the timestamp tamper-proof?
The timestamp is captured at the device level at the moment of recording and is not editable within the app. While no digital timestamp is fully tamper-proof in an absolute legal sense, the metadata is embedded and travels with the file, making alterations detectable.
